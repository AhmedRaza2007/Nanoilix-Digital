A Simple Process to Bring Your Ideas to Life

1

Book a Discovery Call

Schedule a Discovery Call with us to understand your business, project idea, and clarify your vision. We’ll examine your current systems and create actionable next steps while answering any questions you might have about our service.

2

Add Your
Project Details

Once subscribed add your project details to your project board. This means providing all information that will help guide the development process. Don’t worry we’ll walk you through setting up your project board correctly.

3

Create Your
Project Tasks

We breakdown your project details into actionable development tasks. Your developer will then create a weekly ‘Sprint’ cycle comprised of multiple tasks that will be due end of week.

4

Request
Unlimited Tasks & Revisions

We revise each tasks until you’re 100% happy. Once you approve a task, we move onto the next and each week a new sprint will be created consisting of new tasks.

5

Track and Manage
your Progress

Easily track your project's progress on Trello. Review tasks, assign revisions, add comments, and share updates seamlessly.
